## Gaps To Fix

### Past continuous: was/were doing
Status: `needs_work`
Kid-friendly scene: Playground or art table: “What were you doing when I called?” “I was drawing ice cream.”
Suggested chapters: Ch 17, Ch 25, Ch 31
Sample English lines:
- What were you doing when I came?
- I was playing on the slide.
- Maya was drawing a castle.
- We were looking for the ball.

### Past perfect / had been / had gone
Status: `needs_work`
Kid-friendly scene: Theme park/zoo/grandma trip: “Have you been there before?” / “I had been there once.” Keep this later and optional.
Suggested chapters: Ch 20, Ch 23, Ch 31
Sample English lines:
- Have you been to Disneyland before?
- Yes, I had been there once.
- Had you gone on the big ride?
- No, I had not gone on that ride.

### Conditionals: if/then/would
Status: `needs_work`
Kid-friendly scene: Homework/play reward: “If we finish homework, we can play Snake and Ladder.”
Suggested chapters: Ch 20, Ch 22, Ch 23, Ch 30
Sample English lines:
- If you finish homework, we can play.
- If it rains, we will play inside.
- If you told me yesterday, I would have brought the game.

### Relative clauses: who/that/where
Status: `needs_work`
Kid-friendly scene: Classroom/show-and-tell: “The girl who sang is my friend.” “This is the shop where we bought ice cream.”
Suggested chapters: Ch 21, Ch 26, Ch 28, Ch 31
Sample English lines:
- The boy who won the game is my friend.
- This is the place where we ate ice cream.
- The book that I read was funny.

### Comparisons: bigger/newer/like/as/nobody like him
Status: `needs_work`
Kid-friendly scene: Games/food/toys: bigger scoop, smaller car, faster runner, nobody plays like her.
Suggested chapters: Ch 6, Ch 7, Ch 10, Ch 31
Sample English lines:
- My scoop is bigger than yours.
- This car is faster than that car.
- Nobody plays Snake and Ladder like Sara.

### Passive / made by
Status: `needs_work`
Kid-friendly scene: Food/art/festival: “This cake was made by mom.” “This drawing was made by Tara.”
Suggested chapters: Ch 12, Ch 25, Ch 28, Ch 31
Sample English lines:
- This pasta was made by dad.
- This card was made by Meena.
- The laddus were made by grandma.
