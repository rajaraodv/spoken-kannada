# New Curriculum Source Coverage Matrix

Covered: 42 | Partially covered: 0 | Needs work: 0

The new kid curriculum now covers all tracked source concept groups, adds early eating, household command, transport, and measurement chapters, places yesterday/today/tomorrow before mixed tense drills, and explicitly introduces Kannada respectful-vs-casual speech levels. Native-speaker review is still needed before Kannada script/romanization generation.
